Ernesto Abella

Ernesto "Ernie" Corpuz Abella | Former Presidential Spokesperson

General Information

- Presidential Spokesperson of Rodrigo Duterte from June 30, 2016 to October 30, 2017

- Succeeded by Harry Roque

- Became the Undersecretary for Strategic Communications and Research Department of Foreign Affairs from October 27, 2017 to October 8, 2021

- Then ran for president as an independent candidate for the 2022 Elections

History

- Graduated high school in Ateneo de Davao University in 1964

- Graduated with BA in pre-medicine in 1968 from the same school

- Had postgraduate studies in Siliman University and earned master's degree in divinity in 1980

- Co-founded a Davao City-based agricultural products manufacturer around 2002

- Got master's degree in social development in 2005 at Asian Institute of Management
