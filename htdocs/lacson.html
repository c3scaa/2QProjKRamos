Panfilo Lacson

Panfilo "Ping" Morena Lacson Sr. | Senator, former police general

General Information

- Senator from 2001-2013, then again from 2016-present

- Was a presidential candidate last 2004 elections

- Served as a Director General of Philippine National Police (PNP) from 1999-2001

- Ran for president for the 2022 elections under Partido para sa Demokratikong Reporma

History

- Graduated in Philippine Military Academy in 1971

- Postgraduate degree of Master in Government Management from Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila in 1996

- Became chief of Presidential Anti-Organized Crime Task Force (PAOCTF) and PNP chief in 1999-2001

- Became senator in 2001-2013 under Laban ng Demokratikong Pilipino, then again in 2016-present as independent

- Became Presidential Assistant for Rehabilitation and Recovery (PARR) in 2013-2015 due to Typhoon Yolanda

- Ran for president in the 2022 elections with Tito Sotto as vice president, promising stronger Covid-19 responses
